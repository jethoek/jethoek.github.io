Jet Hoek

Post-doctoral researcher at the University of Cologne

jhoek [at] uni-koeln.de

I am a linguist researching discourse-level phenomena from a cognitive perspective, with a specific focus on coherence relations and coreference.

I obtained my PhD from Utrecht University in 2018 (advisors: Ted Sanders, Sandrine Zufferey (University of Bern), and Jacqueline Evers-Vermeul), after which I was a post-doc at the University of Edinburgh (Oct 2018-Sept 2019), working with Hannah Rohde.

I am currently a post-doctoral researcher at the University of Cologne, in the Prominence in Language SFB; C04 project "Referential and conceptual activation in discourse".
